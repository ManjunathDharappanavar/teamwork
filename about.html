About MARC

MARC is designed for businesses and institutions that need accurate, real-time visibility into their inventory, assets, and resources. It reduces manual work and improves operational efficiency.

Asset Management

Track physical and digital assets throughout their lifecycle with complete transparency and control.

Resource Optimization

Monitor usage patterns, avoid wastage, and ensure optimal allocation of organizational resources.

Inventory Control

Maintain accurate stock levels, monitor stock movement, and receive alerts to prevent shortages.
